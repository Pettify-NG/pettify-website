Founder's Story

Titilayo Sunday

CEO / FOUNDER

I grew up with dogs—my dad was a local breeder, and our first family pet, Jet, was more than just a dog; he was my childhood companion. But in 2002, Jet wandered off and never came back. Since then, I've searched for a pet like him—a companion that feels like family.

Finding the right pet shouldn't be difficult, but traditional methods are slow, limited, and inconvenient. The process often involves long searches, word-of-mouth recommendations, or settling for fewer choices due to lack of accessibility. That's why we built Pettify—a platform that connects pet lovers with trusted breeders and vendors nearby, eliminating unnecessary hassle.

Whether you're a remote worker needing a loyal companion or a family looking for the perfect pet, Pettify makes the experience simple, safe, and stress-free. Our platform provides variety, reliability, and ease, ensuring you find not just any pet, but the right one for your home and lifestyle.

My journey started with Jet. Now, through Pettify, we help others find their own Jets—pets that bring love, loyalty, and companionship into our lives.

Pettify is an online marketplace for pet lovers, trusted breeders, pet vendors, and veterinarians. You can buy & sell pets, pet accessories, book vet appointments and many more.
Buyers - Download the Pettify app from Google Play & App Store. Breeders & Vets - Sign up on www.pettify.co to create an account. Simply provide your email or Google sign in to get started.
Download the Pettify mobile app from Google Play or App store. Browse the available pets listed by trusted breeders and sellers on the mobile app. Once you find the right companion, you can make the purchase and enter your delivery address for your pet to be delivered.
If you're a breeder or vendor, you can sign up on the Pettify web app at www.pettify.co and start listing pets for sale. Provide detailed descriptions such as category, breed, description, age and vaccination details and clear beautiful pet photos to attract eager buyers.
Pettify vets sellers and provides a safe platform to connect with breeders, but we recommend always verifying the seller and ensuring the pet's health records before making any purchase.
Pettify supports various payment methods such as direct transfers or payments via debit/credit cards through Paystack.
Refund policies are determined by Pettify only if the received Pet was in bad condition or false pet delivery. We can also refund canceled Vet appointments. Please contact Pettify through email hello@pettify.co directly for any refund requests or help.
If you have any questions or issues, you can contact Pettify support via email at hello@pettify.co or through the app's support section and social media channels.
